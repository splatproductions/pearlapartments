If Center City living doesn’t suit your lifestyle needs, 4415 Main Street, in Manayunk, might be just what you’re seeking. Located in the heart of this “village within a city” 4415 Main Street is at the doorstep of Manayunk’s many shopping and dining opportunities. Plus, its easy access to mass transit and highways makes it accessible to both Center City and the surrounding suburbs.

Units at 4415 Main Street are bi-level and feature updated kitchens and baths, central heat & air. and lots of space, including eat-in kitchens. Apartments at 4415 Main start at $1,250.
