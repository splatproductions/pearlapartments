Whether you’re the “shop till you drop” type, a foodie that needs to be in the middle of Philadelphia’s restaurant scene or a Wharton, Penn or Drexel University student needing close proximity to West Philadelphia, 1512 Walnut offers a unique apartment living opportunity. Located in the heart of Philadelphia’s Rittenhouse Row, 1512 offers the city’s best dining and shopping right outside your door. Besides these obvious draws, though, 1512 is also easily walkable to all of Center City’s workplaces and educational institutions. Additionally, mass transit options abound, making West Philadelphia and the suburbs easily reachable as well.

1512 Walnut’s apartments feature the finishes and amenities Pearl is known for, including stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, high ceilings and hardwood floors. And, with one bedrooms starting at $1,595/month, 1512 offers some of the best value in apartment living in all of Center City.
