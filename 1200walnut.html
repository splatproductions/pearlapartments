Located just east of Walnut Street’s World Class shopping and just west of Washington Square, 1200 Walnut Street offers apartment living in the heart of Center City, Philadelphia. Life at 1200 Walnut puts you close to major subway hubs, Thomas Jefferson University Hospital and the vast shopping, business and educational offerings found in this part of the City.

Sophistication only starts with its location, though. Inside, 1200 Walnut features all new renovation and high end features such as hardwood floors, new central air and heat as well as towering ceilings and walk-in closets. Our spotless new kitchens – featuring stainless steel appliances and granite countertops – will have you thinking about eating in more and eating out less. And each unit has a built-in study area – helping to keep your living space organized and efficient.

Despite its upscale flavor, one bedroom and studio apartments at 1200 Walnut are affordable, with studios starting at $1,395.
