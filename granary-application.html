Frequently Asked Questions

  • Be one of the first to claim a luxury unit at Center City's next great residential address. Reserving now means you will get exactly the type of unit you want, in whatever floor or location you prefer.


  • FAQ

  • When will the new apartments be ready?

    We anticipate that The Granary will be ready for occupancy in July 2013. Opening date is subject to change, should any unanticipated delays occur.

  • Is the Reservation Fee refundable?

    Unfortunately, no. This $250 fee goes towards administrative costs and will be applied to your security deposit later in the process.

  • Is parking available at The Granary?

    Parking is available on a first come, first serve basis. Granary residents will have access to both underground and on-grade parking paid spaces, which can be reserved at the time you sign your lease.

  • Can I tour the Granary before it opens?

    Yes!  All prospective tenants who reserve an apartment will get a pre-construction tour of The Granary in the fall. At that time, prospective tenants will be able to pick their units in the order in which they reserved a unit.
