RENTAL APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Complete the first two pages of the application as completely as possible.
  2. Once your application is completed hit "submit". You will then be directed to the Rentpayment system so you can pay the required fees online.
  3. The deposit required to hold an apartment is one month's rent; a $75 application fee must also be included for each application submitted.
  4. Note that you may only pay via the Rentpayment system if you are a resident of the United States. If you are applying for an apartment from outside the United States and do not have access to a U.S. Bank account then please contact us for instructions on wiring the amount due.
  5. If you are not a U.S. resident, please include a copy of your Graduate School acceptance letter or your Employment Offer.
  6. We will confirm receipt of application and payment within 24 hours.
